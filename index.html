DoomCraft is a browser-based Doom 2 clone, with an added 8th weapon that places and removes Minecraft-style blocks.

You can play Solo, or multiplayer Deathmatch. The Freedoom WAD is included by default for levels, or you can upload a WAD (which will be cached inside your browser for future games).

To host a multiplayer game just click the Host game button and share the generated URL with 1-3 other players — the WAD you select will distribute to the other players. You can opt out of the Minecraft-style block gun if you want.
